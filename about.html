Our Journey Abroad

Our Story

We're three students from Texas Christian University who met while studying abroad in London through the ACCENT Global Learning program at the University of Westminster and quickly became inseparable.

What started as shared classes and navigating a new city turned into something so much bigger. From figuring out the Tube on our first day to traveling across Europe on weekends, we built a friendship and a set of experiences that completely changed how we see travel, independence, and stepping outside our comfort zones.

Study London started as our capstone project, but it quickly became something more meaningful. As TCU students going through the ACCENT program, we realized how overwhelming studying abroad can feel at first — new city, new systems, new everything.

We wanted to create something we wish we had: an interactive platform that makes studying abroad feel easier, less intimidating, and way more exciting.

Whether you're a TCU student heading to London through ACCENT or studying somewhere else entirely, our goal is the same — we want you to feel confident navigating your new environment and to have the kind of experience that stays with you long after you leave.

Why We Made This

Studying abroad is more than just a semester. It is an experience that changes how you see the world and yourself.

As TCU students who had the opportunity to go abroad through ACCENT, we know how impactful that experience can be and how intimidating it can feel at the beginning.

We created this platform to make that transition easier, but also to encourage you to fully embrace it.

Say yes to the trip. Explore the city. Get lost a little. It's all part of it.
