💡 Student Budget Tips

🛒 Save on Groceries

Shop at Tesco, Sainsbury's, or Aldi for budget options. Cook at home to save money compared to eating out every day.

→ Find Grocery Stores on Map

🚇 Tube & Transport

Get a student Oyster card for discounts. A weekly cap with a student card can save you £15-20 per week.

→ View Transport Hubs

☕ Coffee Culture

London has amazing independent cafés. Look for "student discount" spots around campus and your neighborhood.

→ Find Cheap Coffee Shops

🍽️ Free Food Events

Your university often hosts free food events, socials, and networking with free snacks. Check the student portal!

→ View Budget-Friendly Spots

🎟️ Budget Dining

Use your student ID for discounts at restaurants, museums, and attractions. Apps like Student Beans offer exclusive deals.

→ Find Affordable Restaurants

✈️ Free & Cheap Activities

All major museums are free for university students! Plan ahead for budget travel with coach services (National Express, Megabus) for UK trips.
