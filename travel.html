Student-Approved Travel Tips

🧳 Pack the basics

Bring staple clothes and essentials so you do not waste money rebuying basics once you get there.

🍽 Budget for small extras

Some cities charge for things you might not expect, even basics like water, so the total can add up quickly.

🍝 Mix eating out with cooking

Save restaurants for the experiences you really want and make a few meals at home to stretch your budget.

💱 Watch exchange rates

Know the currency conversion before your trip so prices do not feel cheaper than they really are.

🏨 Compare every stay option

The cheapest place to stay changes by city, so compare hostels, Airbnbs, and hotels every time.

👯 Travel with your budget in mind

Trips are more fun when everyone wants the same type of experience and is comfortable with the same spending level.
